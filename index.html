I will now guide you through the tricky process of creating milk steak

What you'll need (Ingredients)

  • 1 medium (8.8oz) juicy piece of meat
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/4 cup honey (Ideally collected by hand from your local hornets nest)
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 of jelly beans (most important part

Step 1: Combine milk, honey, cinnamon, nutmeg, and vanilla to a pan on the stove

Step 2: Heat and stir until honey has disolved. Don't stop stirring to check your match.com

Step 3: Once honey has disolved, allow mixture to boil

Step 4: Place juicy meat in said boiling mixture

Step 5: Bring the liquid back to a boil? I don't think it ever stopped. Anyways, don't even do that, bring the heat DOWN until the milk is just simmering

Step 6: Cook for 5 minutes on each side (there are 6 sides, pick the two biggest). Stir occationally

Step 7: Steak should be cooked hard over, if its only soft boiled, cook for 2 more minutes.

Step 8: Cool steak for 4 minutes on serving dish

Step 9: Garnish with jelly beans
